Remote sensing
================

Here is place for various common and specialized remote sensing tasks and
examples. Remote sensing is what I spend the majority of my time doing. My
primary modalities of remote sensing are in the optical domain; this includes
active laser remote sensing, passive multispectral & hyperspectral, as well as
thermal sensors.
